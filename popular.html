How it Came About

Fortnite in general has been in the making since 2014 but just recently became popular. Since battle royale games are very popular the developers took advantage of it and it's paying off. One major reason it became popular is because it's free (Who wouldn't want free stuff). Also other reasons are that it's constantly updating and changing, Famous people are playing it which helped get the games name out there. Another reason is because no other battle royale game is like fortnite because every other one is get loot and fight but in fortnite you have the ability to build which cause fights to completly change.
